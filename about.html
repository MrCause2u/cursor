About Grace Community Church

Learn about our history, mission, and the people who make our church family special

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Grace Community Church began as a small group of families meeting in a local community center with a shared vision of creating a place where people could experience God's love and grace in a meaningful way.

Over the past 35+ years, we have grown from that small gathering to a vibrant community of believers who are passionate about loving God, loving people, and serving our community. Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness and the dedication of countless individuals who have contributed to our church family.

Today, we continue to build on that foundation while adapting to meet the needs of our modern community, always staying true to our core values of faith, hope, love, and service.

Our Mission

To love God, love people, and serve our community with the grace and truth of Jesus Christ. We strive to create an environment where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, raising up disciples who impact the world for Christ through authentic relationships, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.

Our Values

Faith, Hope, Love, Community, Service, Excellence, and Authenticity. These values guide everything we do and shape how we interact with one another and our community.

What We Believe

Our fundamental beliefs that guide our faith and practice

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our ultimate authority for faith and life.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is the Son of God who died for our sins and rose again to give us eternal life.

Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit guides, comforts, and empowers believers for Christian living.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not by works.

Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and serve.

Great Commission

We believe in sharing the gospel and making disciples of all nations.
