About Aloysius Ministries

Learn about our founding, mission, and Pastor Aloysius who leads this life-changing ministry

Our Story

Founded in 2015 as God's Church, our ministry began with a divine calling to spread the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. Later transformed into Aloysius Prophetic and Healing Ministries, we were established on the solid foundation of Christ's gospel by Pastor Kabagambe Aloyzious (known as Pastor Aloysius).

From the beginning, our ministry has been dedicated to being a life-changing force in the community. We believe in the transformative power of God's word and the importance of teaching children the ways of Christ believers from an early age.

Today, Aloysius Ministries continues to grow and impact lives through our Children's Bible Study Project, which serves over 250 children, providing them with spiritual education, meals, and the foundation they need to grow in Christ.

Our Mission

Aloysius Ministries is a life-changing ministry dedicated to spreading the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. We focus on teaching children the ways of Christ believers and providing them with spiritual foundation, education, and sustenance.

Our Vision

To transform lives through the power of God's word, raise up a generation of children grounded in Christ, and overcome challenges through faith, prophetic ministry, and divine healing power.

Our Values

Gospel Foundation, Children's Ministry, Prophetic Power, Divine Healing, Community Impact, and Faithfulness. We believe in the transformative power of Christ's love in every child's life.

What We Believe

Our fundamental beliefs that guide our faith and practice

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and our ultimate authority for faith and life.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is the Son of God who died for our sins and rose again to give us eternal life.

Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit guides, comforts, and empowers believers for Christian living.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, not by works.

Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and serve.

Great Commission

We believe in sharing the gospel and making disciples of all nations.
